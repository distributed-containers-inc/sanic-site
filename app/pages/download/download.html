Download

Sanic is currently only available for GNU/Linux x64

Download the latest release!

After downloading, take a look at the Example project, as well as the Index tutorial.

You'll also need valid Docker and Kubectl binaries on your path.

To get started, download sanic somewhere on your bin/ directory, i.e., "sudo cp ~/Downloads/sanic /usr/local/bin/"
The first command you'll likely run is "sanic deploy" to start the kubernetes cluster in your local docker, please have patience because this step only ever needs to be slow once!
